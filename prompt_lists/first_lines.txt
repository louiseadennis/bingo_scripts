First Lines
1. It is a truth universally acknowledged
2. It was the best of times, it was the worst of times
3. You shouldn't tell a story till it's over, and I'm not sure this one is.
4. It was a place of ancient evil
5. In the beginning sometimes, I left messages in the street.
6. It was a pleasure to burn.
7. Happy families are all alike; every unhappy family is unhappy in its own way.
8. All this happened, more or less.
9. It was about eleven o'clock in the morning, mid October.
10. If one lives in Galloway, one either fishes or paints.
11. When I was born, my mother took one look at me and laughed.
